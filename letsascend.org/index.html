Changing the Education Landscape

Student-Centered Education (SCE) is education that effectively nurtures the unique gifts and talents of every child. ASCEND's vision is to engage the public in a dialogue around understanding and implementing SCE.

Right now, a group of parents, students, teachers, alumni, and more meet once a month in Marysville, WA in order to discuss SCE and how to create a conversation around education. If you'd like to join us, click the button below!
